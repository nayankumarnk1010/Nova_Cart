About Surana College

The college got accredited by NAAC with 'A' grade in 2013. It is reaccredited with 'A+' followed by grant of 'Autonomous' status under Bangalore University by UGC in 2021. The college has more than 85 Ranks in the university exams and Gold medals, National and International championships in sports and cultural competitions, and high impact research outputs.
These are testament to the continuous improvement in the academic standards at Surana College. Faculty with a blend of academic and industry experience guide students to significantly improve their academic performance and excel in their career. Rooted in Indian culture with a global outlook, the institution is a perfect place for imbibing holistic vision.

The college has been ranked 4th in the state and 28th in the country by the National Institutional Ranking Framework (NIRF) in 2023. It is also ranked 3rd among private colleges in India by the Education World India Private University Rankings 2023. The college has been recognized as a 'College of Excellence' by the University Grants Commission (UGC) in 2018.

Academic Programs: The college offers a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in various disciplines, including engineering, management, commerce, arts, and science.
Infrastructure: The college has state-of-the-art infrastructure,equipped with the latest technology and amenities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped laboratories, a library with a vast collection of books and journals, and sports facilities.
Research & Innovation: The college also has a strong focus on research and innovation, with several research centers and collaborations with industry and academia.

Placement & Alumni: The college has a strong placement record,with students securing jobs in leading companies across various sectors . The alumni network is active and supportive, contributing to the overall development of the institution.
