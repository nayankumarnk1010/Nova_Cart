About City General Hospital

Excellence in healthcare since 1995

Committed to Your Health & Well-being

City General Hospital has been a cornerstone of healthcare excellence in our community for over two decades. We combine cutting-edge medical technology with compassionate care to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

Our team of highly qualified medical professionals, supported by the latest diagnostic and treatment equipment, ensures that every patient receives personalized care tailored to their specific needs.

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of medical care while continuously advancing our capabilities through ongoing research and professional development.
